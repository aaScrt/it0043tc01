About Me

Hi there! I'm Aaliyah Angelica Ramos, 19 years old, currently a freshman at FEU Tech pursuing a degree in BSIT with a major in cybersecurity. While cybersecurity wasn't my initial choice, guided by my parents, I've come to appreciate its potential for a rewarding and impactful career. It equips me with the knowledge and skills to combat cyber threats, ensuring a safer digital environment. Beyond my academic pursuits, I find joy in various hobbies such as volleyball, online games, and listening to music. Oh, and I'm into sketching, playing ukulele, singing, and whipping up delicious dishes, which allows me to express my creativity and enjoy diverse interests. I'm excited about the journey ahead, navigating the dynamic world of technology and cybersecurity, and eagerly embracing opportunities for learning and growth in this fast-evolving field.
